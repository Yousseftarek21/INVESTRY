Create a brand-new Analytics tab for Investry that becomes the user's personal financial intelligence center. Do not overload the page with unnecessary charts or statistics. Instead, focus on presenting meaningful insights with a clean, premium, Apple-inspired interface that feels effortless to understand.
The Analytics tab must follow the latest Apple Human Interface Guidelines (HIG), App Store Review Guidelines, Google Material Design 3, Google Play Developer Policies, and accessibility best practices. Every component should support Light Mode, Dark Mode, Dynamic Type, VoiceOver, RTL (Arabic), and smooth 60–120 FPS animations.
Overall Experience
The Analytics screen should answer three questions:
How is my wealth performing?
Where is my money invested?
What insights can help me make better investment decisions?
The design should feel similar in quality to Apple Stocks, Apple Health, Revolut, Delta Investment Tracker, and TradingView while maintaining Investry's own premium identity.

Final Goal
The Analytics tab should become the most powerful and visually polished financial analytics experience available for investors in Egypt and the MENA region. It should help users understand their financial progress at a glance, monitor wealth growth over time, compare performance against major benchmarks, and receive actionable insights through elegant visualizations. The experience should feel premium, intuitive, and trustworthy—comparable to the quality of Apple Wallet, Apple Stocks, Revolut, Delta Investment Tracker, and TradingView—while remaining uniquely tailored to gold, silver, EGX stocks, real estate, and diversified investment portfolios.
